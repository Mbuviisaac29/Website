Electronics Circuits and Components Overview

During this week‚Äôs session, we explored a broad introduction to various fundamental electronic components.The components discussed were as follows:

-Resistors:- Regulate voltage and current, crucial for setting logic levels in embedded circuits.

-Capacitors:-Stabilize power supply and filter noise in microcontroller systems.

-Inductors:-Support power regulation and signal filtering in embedded power supplies.

-Diodes:-Ensure unidirectional current flow, protecting embedded systems from reverse polarity.

-Relays:-Enable embedded systems to control high-power devices with low-power signals.

-Transistors:-Act as switches or amplifiers for controlling digital logic and interfacing peripherals.

-Quartz Crystals:-Provide precise timing for microcontroller clocks in embedded applications.

Each of these components plays a key role in electronic circuits, and the overview served as a foundation for understanding their individual functions and applications.
